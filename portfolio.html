Rift: Interactive Comic

I lead a team of classmates in a project to turn the quantum theory of superpostion into a comic with interactive elements. This project involved researching a complex field and transforming it into a creative story.

I was in charge of animation-blocking and background design for this project, and also developed C# code for moving between pages and playing video files.

This project was a great opportunity to blend my existing art and animation skills with new experiences in comic-writing, storyboarding, and translating scientific concepts into a creative product.

Skills Used: Background Design, 2D Animation, Comic Storyboarding, Team Leadership, C#, Unity Editor

User-Experience Website Redesign

I redesigned certain pages from the website of Cal Poly Humboldy University, including a redesign and animation of their logo.

This project was an introduction to graphic design with a focus on user-experience principles. During each step of the process, I took user feedback to improve the next iteration

Taking a webpage that I found frustrating to navigate and making changes to it was a welcome challenge for me, and the cycles of feedback and redesign improved my confidence in my design capabilities.

Skills Used: Graphic Design, UX Design, Low- and High-Fidelity Prototyping, Adobe Photostop, Adobe Aftereffects

Card Wizard: AR Game

I worked in a team with classmates to create a 2D Unity game that utilized AR capabilities. The game is a point-and-click shooter in which real-life cards can be scanned each round to give the enemies abilities. One player plays as the wizard, while a second player scans cards to help the enemies win.

This project was my introduction to AR in game design. The cards that I designed had to be remade multiple times because of specifications needed for AR-scanning. I also designed the arenas that each level takes place in, and coded menu screens and scene-switching.

Starting from scratch and designing a 2D environment that interacted with real-life objects to function was a fun challenge to overcome, and allotting different design aspects to each team member led to a fun mixing of art styles.

Skills Used: Pixel Art, Background Design, C#, Augmented Reality, Unity Editor
